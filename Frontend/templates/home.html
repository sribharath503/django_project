Welcome to the Billing Management System

Our billing system provides a seamless way to manage your products, customers, and orders all in one place. It’s designed to help businesses handle day-to-day transactions with accuracy and speed.

In the Products section, you can add, update, and view details about the items you sell, including price and tax information (GST). This ensures that each product is tracked correctly throughout the order process.

The Customers section allows you to manage your customer database — whether you’re onboarding a new customer or updating contact information for existing clients. All customer details are securely maintained for smooth order tracking and billing.

Through the Order Management module, you can create new orders by selecting available products and customers, automatically calculate GST and total billing amounts, and monitor all purchase histories in a structured format.

Whether you're a small retailer or managing bulk orders, this platform simplifies your billing process, improves transparency, and helps you stay organized.
